Bid Table
Avondale scoring — the standard Australian scoring convention. Opponents score 10 pts per trick won regardless of outcome.
Special Bids
Misère
Contract to lose every trick. Worth 250 points. Partner sits out. Bid alone vs the opposing pair.
Open Misère
Same as Misère but after trick 1, your hand is laid face-up. Worth 500 points. Highest possible bid.
Slam Bonus
Win all 10 tricks with a bid worth less than 250? Score 250 points instead of your bid value.
Opponents
Always score 10 points per trick won, regardless of whether the bidding team makes or fails their bid.
How to Play
Australian 500 — 4 players, 2 teams, trick-taking with bidding. First team to 500 wins.
